Assertion: fluvoxamine inhibits cyp2c19

Evidence
Evidence For (item 0) Evidence Type: Non_Tracable_Statement Pointer: fda2006a Reviewer: boycer
Quote: Fluvoxamine is listed as a recommended inhibitor of CYP2C19 for in vivo studies in Table 2 (p. 19). Typically, this would qualify it as a in vivo selective inhibitor however, Table 2 also shows fluvoxamine as a recommended inhibitor of CYP1A2.
