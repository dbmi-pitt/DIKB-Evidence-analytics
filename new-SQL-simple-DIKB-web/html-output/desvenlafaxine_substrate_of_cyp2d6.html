Assertion: desvenlafaxine substrate_of cyp2d6

Evidence
No evidence for!
Evidence Against (item 0) Evidence Type: EV_CT_PK_Genotype Pointer: http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/19142106 Reviewer: boycer
Quote:
Route of administration: oral

polymorphic enzyme: yes

study duration: 100 mg desvenlafaxine on day 1 followed by 120 hours of PK data sampling; repeated on day 11

population: healthy adults, non-smokers (> 1yr), genotyped (7 CYP2D6 extensive metabolizers and 7 poor metabolizers)

ages: 18-55

description:
Results: After administration of venlafaxine ER, mean Cmax and AUC of venlafaxine were significantly greater in PMs compared with EMs, whereas mean Cmax and AUC of its metabolite, desvenlafaxine, were significantly lower for PMs than for EMs (P = 0.001, all comparisons). In contrast, mean Cmax and AUC of desvenlafaxine after administration of desvenlafaxine were comparable between EMs and PMs.
Assumptions: cyp2d6_polymorphic_enzyme_True
