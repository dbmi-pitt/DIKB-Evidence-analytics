Assertion: 7-hydroxyperphenazine maximum_concentration continuous_value

Evidence
Evidence For (item 0) Evidence Type: Non_traceable_Drug_Label_Statement Pointer: perphenazine-sandoz-022008 Reviewer: boycer
Quote: Following oral administration of perphenazine tablets, mean peak plasma perphenazine concentrations were observed between 1 to 3 hours. The plasma elimination half-life of perphenazine was independent of dose and ranged between 9 and 12 hours. In a study in which normal volunteers (n=12) received perphenazine 4 mg q8h for 5 days, steady-state concentrations of perphenazine were reached within 72 hours. Mean (%CV) Cmax and Cmin values for perphenazine and 7-hydroxyperphenazine at steady-state are listed below:

Parameter Perphenazine 7-Hydroxyperphenazine
Cmax (pg/mL) 984 (43) 509 (25)
Cmin (pg/mL) 442 (76) 350 (56)

Peak 7-hydroxyperphenazine concentrations were observed between 2 to 4 hours with a terminal phase half-life ranging between 9.9 to 18.8 hours.
Assumptions:
maximum_concentration: 5.09e-07
dose: 0.004 numb_subjects: None
