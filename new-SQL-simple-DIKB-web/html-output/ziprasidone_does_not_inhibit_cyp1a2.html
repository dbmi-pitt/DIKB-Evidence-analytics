Assertion: ziprasidone does_not_inhibit cyp1a2

Evidence
Evidence For (item 0) Evidence Type: Non_traceable_Drug_Label_Statement Pointer: ziprasidone-roerig-082008 Reviewer: boycer
Quote: An in vitro enzyme inhibition study utilizing human liver microsomes showed that ziprasidone had little inhibitory effect on CYP1A2, CYP2C9, CYP2C19, CYP2D6 and CYP3A4, and thus would not likely interfere with the metabolism of drugs primarily metabolized by these enzymes.
