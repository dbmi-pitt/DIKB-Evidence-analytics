Assertion: ziprasidone maximum_concentration continuous_value

Evidence
Evidence For (item 0) Evidence Type: EV_CT_Pharmacokinetic Pointer: http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/10771448 Reviewer: boycer
Quote:
route of administration: oral

study duration: "Methods Single and multiple doses of ziprasidone were given orally (as two divided daily doses), at fixed dosages of 10 and 40 mg/day, and using titrated regimens of 40 +/- 80 and 40 +/-120 mg/day, for 14 days. All dosages were taken immediately after food.

population: 39 healthy, non-smoking, males. None were tested for known CYP450 phenotypes. "In this double-blind, parallel-group study, subjects were randomised to receive placebo or one of four doses of ziprasidone"

ages: 18 - 49

C_max @ 10mg/day: 14.8ng/mL

14.8ng/mL x 1g/10^9ng x 1000mL/1L = 0.0000148g/L


Assumptions:
maximum_concentration: 1.48e-05
dose: 0.01 numb_subjects: None
